# User Manual – NBA Bets Page

## Viewing matchups

- Click **NBA** in the left sidebar.
- Game cards load automatically for the **current week (Sunday through Saturday)** using **ESPN’s NBA scoreboard** (not only today’s games).
- The page shows a **week badge** and a **date range** for that Sunday–Saturday window.
- Games are **listed under day headings** (e.g. Monday, Mar 10) so you can browse the full week.
- Each card shows the **league and season**, **away @ home** abbreviations, **tip-off time**, **status** (PRE / IN / POST), **scores** when available, **full team names**, and **clock** text when ESPN provides it.

## Searching games

- Use the **search bar** to find games by **team name** or **team abbreviation**.

## Placing a bet (Moneyline / Spread / Total)

- **Moneyline**, **Spread**, and **Total** appear on each game card but are **currently disabled** until an odds API is connected (tooltip explains this).
- When enabled in a future update, you would pick a market, enter stake, review payout, and confirm or cancel—similar to other bet flows in the app.

## Viewing futures and awards

- Click the **Futures** tab at the top of the NBA page.
- **Futures and awards are not loaded from ESPN’s scoreboard**; that tab explains that a **separate odds/futures source** is needed to show markets such as championship or MVP.

---

## Troubleshooting

- If the page shows **“Failed to load NBA matchups from ESPN.”** — ESPN or the network may be temporarily unavailable. **Refresh after ~30 seconds.**
- If you see **“No matchups found”** after loading — check that the **search box is empty**, or that the week actually has scheduled games.
- If the list stays on **Loading…** — check your connection and try a **hard refresh** or another browser.

---

*Matchups and scores: ESPN NBA scoreboard API. Betting lines require a future odds integration.*
